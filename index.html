About NI University In Short

North India University, known as one of the Major University of the Country, has a distinguished history. It has contributed immensely to the development of modern India. The University has been served by many illustrious Faculty. Its notable achievements in academics and research helped the University carving a distinct identity for itself.

Research Scholars

Master degree holders having secured at least 55% of marks in the relevant discipline of any other University can apply. They can do their Ph.D registration provided they qualify through and entrance test followed by an interview.
